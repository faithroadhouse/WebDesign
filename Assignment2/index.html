Twin Peaks: Fire Walk With Me

directed by Mark Frost and David Lynch

Preface

Twin Peaks: Fire Walk With Me is the prequel to the TV series Twin Peaks, directed by Mark Frost and David Lynch. The story follows the beautifully tragic life of teenager Laura Palmer before her death, which was revealed to us in the first episode of the TV series.

Act 1

We start out with a parallel. We see the body of Teresa Banks floating down a river and then we cut to investigators doing their investigative work. At the same time, Laura Palmer and her best friend Donna Hayward are at school.

Act 2

We start to see the role BOB plays in Laura’s life. BOB isn’t technically a physical person, but more of a spirit that has to find a host. BOB is mentally torturing Laura. He wants to use her as a vessel, but she won’t let him.

Act 3

Through the TV series, we already know who BOB really was, but this is where we start to see Laura figure it out. In a tragic scene, she finally realizes that BOB has taken over her father.
