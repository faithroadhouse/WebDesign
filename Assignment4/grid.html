The Main Character

Kyle MacLachlan as Dale Cooper of the F.B.I

Information

Born on April 19th, 1954 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Cooper loves coffee and cherry pie. Also has some special interests in Tibet.

His role

Dale Cooper's role in Twin Peaks is to investigate the mysterious murder of Laura Palmer, and get to the bottom of who was involved.

His Collegues

Sherrif Truman, Deputy Andy, Deputy Hawk, Lucy, and Gordon Cole.
