Twin Peaks: Fire Walk With Me

directed by David Lynch

Preface

Twin Peaks: Fire Walk With Me is the prequel to the TV series Twin Peaks. The story follows the tragic life of teenager Laura Palmer before her death, which is revealed in the first episode of the series.

Act 1

We start with a parallel between Teresa Banks' death and the investigation that follows, while Laura Palmer and Donna Hayward go about their daily lives at school.

Act 2

We begin to see the role BOB plays in Laura’s life. He is not a typical physical person, but a spirit seeking a host. He psychologically torments Laura and tries to possess her, but she resists.

Act 3

As the story unfolds, Laura comes to a devastating realization about BOB’s identity, leading to one of the film’s most tragic moments.
