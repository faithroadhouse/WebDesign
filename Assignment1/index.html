Twin Peaks: Fire Walk with Me

a movie by David Lynch

Description

A prequel to the TV series Twin Peaks, this movie explored the tragic and doomed life of teenage girl Laura Palmer as she navigates how to deal with the curse of BOB. BOB torments her mentally, but none of her friends and family are aware of her struggles.

Other movies by David Lynch

David Lynch has directed many other sensational movies including Lost Highway, the original Dune, Mulholland drive, Blue Velvet, and many others including some short films. All of these are incredibly well written and thought out.
